
# The Meal Planner
Explore now at [foodu8.com](https://foodu8.com)

## Why Does This Exist?
Tired of spending hours every week deciding what to cook and combining all the required ingredients, I sought to develop a solution. Instead of endlessly scrolling through a notepad app on my phone, I decided to streamline the process through a simple web app.

## What Does It Do?
The meal planner offers users a convenient platform to catalog their favorite recipes. When it's time for grocery shopping, users can select the meals they plan to make for the week. The tool then compiles all the necessary ingredients, making the shopping trip efficient and hassle-free.

## How Do I Use It?
Just log in with your Google account and start creating some recipes!
